Homemade Checkers

    Details about the project

  • Created from scratch using no libaries.
  • Persistance managed by a Postgres database
  • Allows users to play to hop into previous unfinished games
  • Allows players to rewind their moves.

    • Rules of Checkers

  • Red Moves First
  • Pieces can only move on the diagonals
  • Red pawns can only move on the up diagonals and black on the down diagonals
  • You must capture all of your opponents pieces in order to win
  • A player captures pieces by hopping over their opponents piece onto an empty tile in the direction they're permitted to travel
  • Upon reaching the opposite side of their origin, pawns become "kings" and my travel on all four diagonals
  • Upon capturing an opponent, if an immediate opportunity for the piece to continue capturing is present, the subsequent capture must occur.
